Beta Bank Successfully Hosts Green Hackathon 2024

On February 7, 2024, Beta Bank successfully hosted the Green Hackathon 2024, an event dedicated to developing sustainable and eco-friendly solutions. The hackathon aimed to foster innovative ideas and support the advancement of environmentally conscious technologies.

Event Highlights:

Date and Location:
Date: February 7, 2024
Location: Beta Bank Headquarters, [City Name]

Participants:
The event saw participation from 150 individuals, including students from various universities, software developers, engineers, and environmental technology experts.

Objectives and Achievements:
The hackathon's primary objective was to create innovative projects focused on sustainable banking and eco-friendly financial solutions.
Participants worked on addressing environmental challenges and promoting sustainability through their projects.

Projects and Winners:
A total of 20 teams developed various projects, which were assessed by an expert jury.
Winning projects included a mobile app that encourages recycling, financial planning tools for energy savings, and eco-friendly investment platforms.
The first-place team was awarded 10,000 TL by Beta Bank and received mentorship support for further project development.

Future Outlook:
Beta Bank plans to continue supporting eco-friendly initiatives and make the Green Hackathon an annual event.

Acknowledgements:
Beta Bank expresses its sincere gratitude to all participants, jury members, and supporters of the Green Hackathon 2024. We are proud to have collaborated on projects that contribute to a sustainable future.
